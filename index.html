About Me

I am a recent grad of a competitive art program from the university of Arizona. I received a BFA in Visual communications. Creative problem solving has become my bread and butter. Recently taking on the challenges of learning coding. I want the world of fine art and raw codes to be brought together to make functional fun art.

I’m not sure what I live for more completing a challenge and showing that I did and can do it or answering the answerable. Challenges never stop coming I’ve overcome them repeatedly.
